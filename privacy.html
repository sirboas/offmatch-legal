Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Agentis Technologies Inc ("OffMatch," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the OffMatch mobile application (the "App"). OffMatch is a platform that connects principals in off-market commercial real estate ("CRE") transactions: sellers post anonymized property profiles, buyers express interest, and when both sides agree, a private chat opens.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information you provide directly

Account information (all users):

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored only as a one-way encrypted hash — we never store or see your actual password)
  • Account role (buyer or seller)

Optional profile information:

  • Phone number
  • Firm or company name
  • Professional license number

Listing information (sellers):

When you post a property, you provide details such as asset type, market and submarket, size, price, cap rate, NOI, year built, occupancy, tenancy type, deal timeline, a short description, and an optional cover photo. You may also provide the property's street address and owner name. These last two fields are private — they are stored on your own listing record and are never shown to other users through the App. They are only ever shared with another party if you choose to share them yourself in chat.

Messages:

The content of chat messages (and any attachments you send) you exchange with a matched counterparty after a confirmed match.

b) Information collected automatically

  • Activity data — the listings you swipe on (interest or pass), the matches you create, and your message history. This is core to how the matching service works.
  • Device push token — if you enable notifications, we store a push token so we can send you alerts about new interest, matches, and messages.
  • Technical/log data — your IP address and request timestamps, used by our servers for security, abuse prevention, and rate limiting.
  • Session tokens — we store secure tokens to keep you logged in.

We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information above to:

  • Create and manage your account and authenticate you
  • Operate the core matching service (show listings, record swipes, detect matches, open chat on a confirmed match)
  • Display limited profile information to a counterparty you have matched with (see Section 3)
  • Send you push notifications about activity relevant to you (you can turn these off in your device settings at any time)
  • Keep the platform secure — detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access, and enforce rate limits
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

3. How Information Is Shared

With other users (limited, and only on a match): OffMatch is built around anonymity until both sides agree. Until a match is confirmed, buyers see only the anonymized listing — never the street address, owner name, or seller's contact details. When a match is confirmed, a private chat opens between the two parties. Identifying details (such as exact address, names, firm, license number, or contact info) are exchanged only at each party's own discretion within that chat — the App does not auto-reveal them.

With service providers (subprocessors) who run the platform for us:

  • Railway — application hosting
  • Supabase — database hosting and file storage (listing cover photos)
  • Expo Push Service, and through it Apple Push Notification service (APNs) and Google Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) — delivery of push notifications to your device

These providers process data only to provide their service to us and are bound by their own privacy and security obligations.

For legal reasons: We may disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or other legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of OffMatch, our users, or the public.

In a business transfer: If OffMatch is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. When a listing or match is removed, we use a "soft delete" — the record is marked deleted and hidden from the App, and is purged or retained only as needed for security, dispute resolution, and legal compliance.

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time by emailing us at the address in Section 9. We will delete or anonymize your personal information except where we are required to retain it by law.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Receive a copy of your information in a portable format

To exercise any of these, email us at the address in Section 9. We will respond as required by applicable law.

Push notifications: You can disable them at any time in your device's notification settings.

California / other US state residents: We do not sell or "share" (as defined under the CCPA) your personal information. You may exercise the access and deletion rights described above without discrimination.

6. Security

We protect your information using industry-standard measures, including: one-way password hashing (bcrypt), encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit, JWT-based authentication with rotating refresh tokens, security headers, and rate limiting on sensitive endpoints. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information and limit access to it.

7. Children's Privacy

OffMatch is intended for commercial real estate professionals and is not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, provide notice in the App. Your continued use of the App after a change means you accept the updated policy.
